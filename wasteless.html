Wasteless Web-App

Description

The purpose of the Wasteless-App is to manage groceries and quickly find a matching recipe without wasting food. Search or click through saved recipes to find one, which has the most ingredients in common with your grocery list. The main functionality of the app is, that items in the grocery list are compared with ingredients of added recipes. On the recipe card, green symbols indicate that an ingredient is in the grocery list and orange symbols indicate that an ingredient is in the grocery list, but with a lesser amount. So you easily know what's left to buy. On the grocery site you can add food items to different food categories. Most food items are saved in a datalist, which quickens the adding process. As a design feature the food items are rendered with a small matching icon. Each item, that is added with an amount, has an input and button to change the amount of that items instantly. The recipe form allows to add recipes with as many ingredients as needed. The recipes are added to predefined categories. You also specify a source for the recipe (for example a cookbook or a website link), so that you know where to find it. When clicking on a recipe category, all added recipes for that category are shown in a sorted order. The recipe with the most ingredients available in the grocerieslist is shown at the top. The order changes when matching food items are added or deleted in the groceries list.

Languages used:

React/Redux, Javascript, CSS, HTML
