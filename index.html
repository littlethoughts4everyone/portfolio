About Me

Hello, I'm Manuel, a programmer and audio engineer based in Hamburg, Germany. Since 7 years I've been working at a dubbing studio, where I record, edit and mix the audio for german versions of international feature films, tv-series and documentaries.

In search of new challenges and the curiosity to learn something new, I've started programming two years ago. My main interests are music (especially in the indie-alternative-genre), movies, playing the guitar, travelling and cooking.

I've recently started working on my own music database website, that is focusing on the lyrical content and trivia of songs from a wide range of bands and artists. The aim of the website is for a user to find songs matching specific themes and moods, making it easier for them to create their own unique playlists. On the side will be some interesting trivia and technical knowledge about the songs and their associated albums. There will also be an interactive section, where users can vote for and comment on songs with the same theme. Another section should randomly show the best quotes from all of the songs on a daily basis.

In the future I want to constantly improve my programming skills and take a new job in the professional programming field and work with a team on interesting projects. I'll also look for collaborators, who are interested in helping me with my music website and want to add content to it.
